What is the DBOT Program?

The Dragon Boat Officer-in-Training program was created in 2006. The DBOTs program will open your eyes to the leadership involved with this team, but beyond that is the opportunity for you to grow as a person, as a leader, and as a paddler. You'll learn, see, and experience things that unique to dragon boat and it is truly worthwhile from the memories and bonds that you create.

Applications released Winter Quarter of each year.
