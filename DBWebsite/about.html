Who are we?

UCI Elements, a collegiate team from the University of California, Irvine, is going into its tenth year . We have participated in various races from Arizona to San Diego to the Bay Area. As the Elements, we are represented by the elements wind, thunder, and fire. Our team is built upon hard work, passion, and strong team bonds. We thrive and depend on friendships and close bonds made on the team to make this team into a close family.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm not athletic?

Training with our team will get you stronger physically and mentally.

What are fitness tests for and who takes them?

To determine relative fitness and determine who gets to be on a boat. Limited space for races These tests are not the only measure for boat determination

What are the costs?

New member fee: $30
- Arc, boat rentals, equipment rentals, practice jersey (first-come-first-serve, sizing is limited)

How many races do you have a year?

We have one race per quarter, and two during the summer.

What equipment do I need?

You don't need anything at open practices, but if you wish to continue, you will need a paddle and your own PFD (personal flotation device).
